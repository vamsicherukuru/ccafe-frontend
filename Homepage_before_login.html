BUILDING NEIGHBOURHOODS INTO COMMUNITIES

Community Café aims to bring a “Community” closer with a view to build a credible support system, provide a platform to interact and network on common interests and find ways to contribute in a meaningful way to the larger Community and its identified social causes. The name “Community Café” is to denote the online avatar of the old fashioned, brick and mortar “friendly neighborhood café” where members of a Community meet and interact in an informal and friendly setting, building relationships and support systems.



How it Works

We believe that our society is in critical need of a more responsible, ethical, trustworthy platform for essential community services and products.

FIND LOCAL

We are focused on a hyper-local business model of an online community center in the heart of every neighborhood and actively seek their participation In creating trustworthy services and healthy products for the community. The platform looks to nurture talent and entrepreneurs from within the community and build local business through professional mentoring and a collaborative ecosystem across other communities in the city and beyond to impact local initiative and enterprise.

Frequently Asked Questions

We know there's a lot to learn and read about all the integration points of the platform. That's why we're giving you this. More to read, easier to skim.

  • About Community Cafe
    • What is Community Cafe?

      Our community is a place where we belong. Community reflects working together for a common purpose. Café is a platform where we can interact and network to share and learn from each other. The Community Café is bring the community closer through the Mobile App and build a credible support system and find ways to contribute in a meaningful way to the larger community and social causes.

    • Whom is it meant for?

      The community café is meant for any community of like-minded people who share common interests – a geographic or virtual community – where you want to create a support system of trustworthy services and quality relationships.

    • Who can join?

      Are you currently part of a community? Are you looking for a better way to communicate, find members, and collaborate? Then join us!

    • Why should I join Community Cafe?

      Want to contribute to your community by hosting events, or have a civic issue to take care off, or just want to find others with similar interests? Community Cafe provides a platform for community members to Find, Belong and Grow in their respective communities. For more information about us visit our website and learn more.

    • Why should a community join us?

      Is your community looking for:
      1. An easier way to communicate with your community members without the hassle of getting lost in the clutter of irrelevant messages.
      2. Create more belonging and interaction with the community through interests, events and engaging conversations
      3. Foster creativity, collaboration, and entrepreneurship within your community. Of the community! By the community! For the community!
      4. Enjoy no maximum member restrictions and create inclusivity so that the whole community can be on one platform.
      5. One place to provide the whole community with phone numbers, and contact details instead of typing them over and over again.

    • Where are we available?

      Currently we are only available in India, but hope to expand across the globe to every community!

    • What devices can we be downloaded on?

      Right now we are a mobile app, and can be used only on Mobile phones. To download please visit us on Google PlayStore, or Apple IPhone by searching for Community Cafe. We will also be releasing a web version.

    • Where to download the app?

      Our app can be downloaded on both Androids on Google PlayStore, and Apple IPhone AppStore by searching for Community Cafe.

    • Is the app free?

      The app is free for all members to download and use. Any additional charges will be based on transactions or services that you require. Although, some communities might require their members to pay a membership fee first, so that they can get their membership number and be validated. This fee is important as it goes back into the community for events, civic issues, general maintenance.

  • How to Get Started ?
    • Find your community

      Whether you are looking to resolve your local civic issues, or find community members with similar interests and hobbies, all you have to do is:
      ■ Step 1: Download community cafe from Google Playstore, or Apple App Store
      ■ Step 2: Fill in your details and select your community
      ■ Step 3: Start Posting! And discover our other features that help you Find, Belong and Contribute to your community.

    • Find your interests

      Want to join a group of foodies to discuss the best take-away places in the community, or interested in gardening and looking for other horticulturalists around you?
      ■ Step 1: Join Community Cafe
      ■ Step 2: Join your community and become a verified member
      ■ Step 3: Click interest
      ■ Step 4: Select your interest, and get posting!
      If you cannot find the interest group you want, click on request a group so we can create it for you.

    • Add members to your community

      To add members to your community, simply ask friends and neighbours to:
      ■ Step 1: Download the app from Google Playstore, or the Apple AppStore.
      ■ Step 2: Fill their details and join the appropriate community.
      ■ Step 3: Get validated by a Community Admin.
      ■ Step 4: Find you and start communicating!.
      For any issues write to us at support@communitycafe.in

    • How to visit Helpdesk?

      Our helpdesk is a directory of all numbers you might need to solve any issues you might have in the community. From verified local plumbers, and carpenters to fixing street lights and other issues. It also has a list of CWA members who are available for emergency calls.
      ■ Step 1: Open your app
      ■ Step 2: Click HelpDesk
      ■ Step 3: Find the number you want and click Call!

    • Will I receive notifications?

      Like a silent airport we too don’t make unnecessary announcements that might add to your ‘social media message pollution’. However, for events, charity, and emergency situations members will receive a notification.

    • How to add photos and videos?

      ○ Step 1: Open your app
      ○ Step 2: Decide where you want to post - interest group/community board etc.
      ○ Step 3: Click the camera icon
      ○ Step 4: Upload photo from camera, or gallery
      ○ Step 5: POST!

    • How to share posts?

      ○ Step 1: Open your app
      ○ Step 2: Decide where you want to post - interest group/community board etc.
      ○ Step 3: Type your post and click POST!

    • Events Calendar

      ○ Step 1: Open your app
      ○ Step 2: Click more
      ○ Step 3: Open your bulletin
      ○ Step 4: Click events and check out what’s happening in your community schedule!

  • How to use Community Cafe?
    • How to build your own community on the app?

      ○ It’s simple! Just email us at support@communitycafe.in and tell us the name of your community.
      ○ Let us know if you can verify your community members or have a current process so we can validate and do a background check.

    • Collaborate with Community Cafe

      Are you interested in working with us and helping us expand our vision for communities around the globe? Then write to us at support@communitycafe.in and tell us how you would like to collaborate.

    • Starting a business

      Recently learned to bake banana bread and see a business opportunity, or having entrepreneurial ideas in your interest group. Start the conversation and see how your community reacts to your ideas.

    • How to give back to the community

      Know a local charity that needs help, or a hyper local business suffering because of this pandemic. Post about them, hold auctions and find volunteers so that you can give back to the community.

    • Support hyper-local business

      Have specialised skills and think those around you might benefit from them. Write about them in the interest group — Skills, or other specialised gifts, and help your community succeed.

  • Safety, Privacy and Community Cafe Policies
    • Will my data be safe/private ?

      We take utmost care and precautions to ensure your personal data and privacy is protected. At no point will your data be shared without your approval, acceptance, or consent. However, data security is limited to the extent of internet access, which may not be in our purview

    • Our safety policy

      Our vision is to foster conversations and collaborations within communities, and build safe networks. Any activity or communication that does not serve the interest of the community by inciting divisiveness, polarisation, fraud or other unethical behaviour will be met with necessary action including termination of membership from our app.

    • Verification of members

      ○ Having community members is key to our app and therefore validation will be a requirement.
      ○ Once you join you might be asked to either type in your membership number as given by your CWA or community committee. If not, we will carry out a check and may request for further proof if required.
      ○ If you want to join as a business or service provider you must meet our criteria and must be recommended by at least 2-3 registered community members. If you cannot meet these criteria you will be disabled from the app.

    • Can other communities see me and my community?

      No, each community is created as its own ecosystem and can only be accessed by validated members.

    • Can I quit community cafe?

      Yes, you have the option to quit our app, and uninstall us.

    • Can I join multiple communities?

      Yes, you can do so. However, you must provide authentic identity verification in each different community and must be validated and verified for each.
